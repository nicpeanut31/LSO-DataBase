LSO CONTRACT MAKER UPDATE
=========================

A new Contract section has been added to the website.

FUNCTIONS
---------
- Lists active official members in the Membership Period.
- Search by name, Membership ID, or Student Number.
- Clicking a member automatically generates a two-page contract preview.
- Member Name and Home Address are filled from the member database.
- Postal Address can be corrected for the contract without changing the member record.
- Contract Date defaults to the current date and may be changed.
- Officer in Charge is entered manually.
- Download Contract PDF creates and automatically downloads the completed official PDF.
- The uploaded LSO-CONTRACT.pdf is used as the unchanged official base template.
- Staff Accounts cannot generate contracts; Administrator access is required.

FILES ADDED
-----------
contract-maker.js
contract-maker.css
pdf-lib.min.js
lso-contract-template.pdf

No Supabase SQL update is required because contracts are generated from existing member records and are not stored in the database.
