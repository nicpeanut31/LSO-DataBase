LSO CONTRACT TERM AND PREVIEW UPGRADE
=====================================

WHAT CHANGED
------------
1. The generated Name and Postal Address block on page 1 is centered.
2. The generated Name and Postal Address are bold and underlined.
3. Contract Term is now manually entered through two required fields:
   - Semester
   - Academic Year
4. The manually entered Semester and Academic Year replace the fixed term in Clause 1.
5. The generated Semester and Academic Year are bold and underlined in the PDF.
6. The live PDF preview is now a full-width panel below the roster and form.
7. The preview height was increased for clearer viewing of the two A4 pages.
8. The Open PDF control is renamed Open Large Preview.

INSTALLATION
------------
Replace all website files in GitHub with the contents of this folder.
No Supabase SQL update is required.
After GitHub Pages deploys, press Ctrl + F5.

NEW REQUIRED INPUTS
-------------------
Before previewing or downloading a contract, enter:
- Contract Date
- Officer in Charge
- Semester, for example: 2nd Semester
- Academic Year, for example: 2026-2027

The member Name and Address still come from the selected member record. The address remains editable for the contract.
