LSO DUTY HOURS — ARCHIVE AND WORKFLOW FIX
=========================================

WHAT WAS FIXED
--------------
1. Duty Hours now separates Active Records and the Duty Archive.
2. Trainee records move to the archive when the member enters Probationary or Membership Period.
3. Probationary records move to the archive when the member enters Membership Period.
4. Archived records remain searchable, editable, printable, and included in all totals.
5. Members who skip Probationary Period receive a "Skipped • Archived" Probationary duty ledger.
6. Committed time, rendered clock entries, incentives, semester totals, and academic-year totals continue to include skipped or archived ledgers.
7. Member Registration now includes a Skip the Probationary Period checkbox.
8. The Duty Hours layout was widened and reorganized so forms, tables, and action buttons are not clipped.
9. Overall Duty Hours reports now show whether each record is Active, Archived, or Skipped • Archived.

ARCHIVE RULES
-------------
- Current Trainee: Trainee record is Active.
- Current Probationary: Probationary record is Active; Trainee record is Archived.
- Membership Period: Trainee and Probationary records are Archived.
- Membership Period with Probationary skipped: Trainee record is Archived and the Probationary ledger is marked Skipped • Archived.

IMPORTANT
---------
Archive means the record is categorized as historical. The data is not moved to a separate database table and is never deleted. This keeps all previous duty calculations intact and avoids migration/data-loss risks.

SUPABASE
--------
No new Supabase SQL is required for this update because the existing member and duty_hours JSON structures already support these fields. Upload all updated website files to GitHub and force-refresh the deployed page.
