WHAT WAS ADDED
--------------
- Duty Hours lookup inside Attendance.
- Separate Trainee and Probationary committed-hour targets.
- Rendered duty-hour entries with date and description.
- Positive or negative incentive/adjustment entries.
- Automatic credited and remaining-hour calculations.

- Overall organization totals and member-by-member balances.
- Printable individual and overall Duty Hours reports.
- Duty Hours included in cloud synchronization and Complete Backups.

current members, events, attendance, accounts, or settings.

4. Commit the changes and wait for GitHub Pages to redeploy.
5. Open the live website and press Ctrl + F5.
6. Log in using SNA1161 / SNA1161.

USING DUTY HOURS
----------------
1. Open Attendance.
2. In Duty Hours Lookup, search and select a member.
3. Enter the Trainee and Probationary committed hours, then save.
4. Add an entry:
   - Rendered Duty Hours: use a positive number.
   - Incentive / Adjustment: a positive number reduces the remaining hours;
     a negative number adds required hours back.
5. The system automatically shows:
   - committed hours
   - rendered duty hours
   - net incentives
   - total credited hours
   - remaining or excess hours
6. Use Print Individual or Print Overall to generate an A4-ready report.
