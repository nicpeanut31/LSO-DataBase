LSO DUTY HOURS — CURRENT ROSTER AND MONTHLY PRINT UPDATE
=========================================================

NEW PRINT BUTTONS
-----------------
Trainee Period:
- Print Current Trainees
- Print Monthly Report

Probationary Period:
- Print Current Probationary
- Print Monthly Report

CURRENT ROSTER REPORTS
----------------------
These reports list only members who are currently in the applicable period.
They include member IDs, academic and contact information, period start date,
committed time, rendered time, incentives, credited time, balance, entry count,
and progress for the selected semester.

MONTHLY REPORTS
---------------
Choose a month in the Duty Hours Print Center before printing. Monthly reports:
- respect the selected First or Second Semester
- remain separate for Trainee and Probationary members
- include all current members in that period, even when they have no entries
- calculate rendered time, incentives, credited time, and duty-session count
  only from the selected month
- include a detailed monthly ledger
- show semester committed, credited-to-date, and remaining balance for context

ARCHIVED RECORDS
----------------
Archived records are not included in the Current roster or Monthly reports.
They remain accessible in Duty Archive and can still be selected and printed
through the Individual Duty Hours Report.

DATABASE
--------
No Supabase SQL change is required. This update changes presentation and report
logic only and keeps the existing configured shared database connection.
