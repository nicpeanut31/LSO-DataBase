LASALLIAN SYMPHONY ORCHESTRA
ATTENDANCE FINALIZATION, AUDIT HISTORY, ANALYTICS, ALERTS, AND REPORTS

WHAT WAS ADDED

1. Draft and Finalized Attendance
- Every attendance roster begins as Draft.
- Draft attendance can be edited and saved by an Administrator.
- Finalize & Lock Attendance completes the roster and prevents further changes.
- Any unmarked member is automatically recorded as Absent during finalization.
- Finalization is stored separately for each Event, Attendance Group, and Roster Mode.
  Example: Official Members Current Roster is separate from Trainee Members Current Roster.

2. Administrator Editing of Locked Attendance
- A finalized roster displays Unlock for Editing.
- The Administrator must enter a correction reason.
- The roster returns to Draft and becomes editable.
- After corrections are saved, the Administrator must finalize it again.
- Each re-finalization increases the attendance revision number.

3. Attendance Audit History
The system records:
- Draft attendance saves
- Finalization date and Administrator
- Automatic Absent entries created during finalization
- Unlock date, Administrator, and correction reason
- Re-finalization revisions

The audit history is shown inside the selected attendance roster and can be exported as CSV.

4. Individual Analytics
For the selected member, the website now displays:
- Working Rate: Draft and Finalized records
- Verified Rate: Finalized records only
- On-Time Rate: Present divided by Present plus Late
- Consecutive Absences
- Total Late records
- Draft events that are not yet verified
- Current attendance risk signals

5. Attendance Alerts
The Action Center can now flag:
- Past events with no attendance records
- Past attendance that remains Draft
- Attendance unlocked for correction but not re-finalized
- Two or more consecutive absences
- Three or more Late records
- Attendance below the configured threshold

An event alert includes an Open Attendance button.

6. Reports and Exports
- Semester and monthly printable reports include the attendance verification state.
- Individual printable reports show whether each event is Draft or Finalized.
- Export Analytics CSV creates a group-level attendance analytics report.
- Export Member CSV creates an event-by-event member attendance report.
- Export Audit CSV creates a selected-roster audit report.

INSTALLATION

1. Upload and replace the website files using this package.
2. Keep the same folder structure.
3. No Supabase SQL update is required for this feature.
4. The workflow and audit information are stored inside the existing Events JSON data, so the current database schema remains compatible.
5. After deployment, press Ctrl + Shift + R.
6. If the installed PWA still shows an older version, close it completely and reopen it. The service-worker cache version has been updated.

RECOMMENDED TEST

1. Log in as Administrator.
2. Open Attendance and select a past event.
3. Mark at least one member and save the Draft.
4. Select Finalize & Lock Attendance.
5. Confirm that unmarked members become Absent and all controls are locked.
6. Open the audit history.
7. Select Unlock for Editing and enter a reason.
8. Correct a record, save it, and finalize again.
9. Select an individual member to review Working Rate, Verified Rate, risk signals, and reports.
10. Open Action Center to review attendance alerts.

IMPORTANT

Finalized attendance can still be corrected, but only through the Administrator's Unlock for Editing action. This ensures that every correction has an identified Administrator, timestamp, reason, and revision history.
