DUTY ROSTER READABILITY FIX
===========================

This update rebuilds the Trainee and Probationary roster card headers instead
of layering more CSS over the earlier layout.

Changes:
- Full stage titles are visible and may wrap naturally.
- Count badges have a fixed, matching position.
- Report buttons are placed in their own dedicated row.
- Button wording is split into readable two-line labels.
- Both cards use matching spacing, dividers, heights, and empty states.
- Mobile and narrower desktop layouts stack the report buttons cleanly.

No Supabase SQL change is required.
