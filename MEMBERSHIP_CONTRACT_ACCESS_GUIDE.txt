LSO MEMBERSHIP CONTRACT FULL ACCESS UPDATE

Purpose
-------
This update gives the Membership role full access to the Contract workspace.

Membership role permissions in Contract
---------------------------------------
- Open the Contract module.
- Search and select an active official member.
- Review and correct the postal address for the generated contract.
- Enter the contract date, officer in charge, semester, and academic year.
- Generate and refresh the live two-page contract preview.
- Open the large preview.
- Download the completed membership contract PDF.
- Clear and prepare another contract.

Unchanged restrictions
----------------------
- Staff Account, General Secretary, and Trainee/Probationary cannot generate contracts.
- Account management and other Administrator-only functions remain restricted.
- No Supabase SQL update is required because contract generation is performed in the website and the Membership role already has access to the Contract module.

Deployment
----------
1. Upload every file from this folder to the GitHub Pages repository.
2. Keep index.html in the publishing root.
3. Do not rename files or allow GitHub to add suffixes such as (1).
4. Open the deployed website and press Ctrl + Shift + R.
5. If installed as a PWA, close the app completely and reopen it once.
6. Sign in with a Membership account and test Preview, Open Large Preview, and Download Contract PDF.

The active cache-safe files are:
- contract-maker-membership-v4.js
- pwa-membership-contract-v9.js
- service-worker-membership-contract-v10.js
