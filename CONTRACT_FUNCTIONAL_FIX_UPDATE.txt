LSO CONTRACT MAKER — FUNCTIONAL AND DISPLAY FIX
================================================

WHAT WAS FIXED
--------------
1. Removed the contract template network dependency that caused "Failed to fetch".
   The official two-page contract template is now embedded in
   contract-template-data.js. The original PDF file remains included as a
   fallback.

2. Fixed contract generation and direct PDF downloading.
   - Select an Official Member.
   - Review or enter the Postal Address.
   - Choose the Contract Date.
   - Enter the Officer in Charge.
   - Click Download Contract PDF.

3. Fixed the live preview.
   - The preview is generated from the same completed PDF that is downloaded.
   - An Open PDF button is shown after successful generation.
   - If the browser cannot display an inline PDF, the Open PDF button and the
     Download Contract PDF button remain available.

4. Rebuilt the Contract workspace layout.
   - Member names can wrap to two lines and are not covered by avatars or
     address labels.
   - Address status is shown below the member identity.
   - The form buttons no longer overlap.
   - The preview panel uses a larger, responsive document area.
   - The command header now has readable contrast.
