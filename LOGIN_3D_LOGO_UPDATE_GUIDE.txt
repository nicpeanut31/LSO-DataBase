LSO LOGIN 3D LOGO UPDATE

WHAT CHANGED
- The login logo is rendered as a layered 3D card through HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
- The original LSO logo remains the source artwork; no pre-rendered 3D image is used.
- The design includes physical-looking depth layers, dynamic lighting, an idle float, a moving highlight, and pointer-based tilt on desktop.
- Motion is reduced automatically for users who enable Reduce Motion.
- Tablet and mobile layouts use a lighter, compact 3D presentation.

DEPLOYMENT
1. Upload every file in this folder to the GitHub Pages publishing root.
2. Keep index.html in the root.
3. Press Ctrl + Shift + R after GitHub Pages finishes deploying.
4. If the site is installed as a PWA, close it completely and reopen it once.
